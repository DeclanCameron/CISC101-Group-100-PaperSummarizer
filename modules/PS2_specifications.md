## PS2 Specifications
In addition to the specification in the system prompt and each module of the internal reference framework, you must include and follow these initial specifications as a base ground framework:

Inputs:		The full text or sectioned content (Abstract, Introduction, Methods, Results, Discussion, Conclusion)

Outputs:		Structured summary in list format. Each section has their corresponding heading and is summarized into key ideas below in paragraph form.

Constraints:		For every section that is included in the input, the summary must contain exactly one accurate key idea that correctly reflects the main point of that section. The final summary must be under 200 words.
